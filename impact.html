Who Is Responsible?

This issue is under the responsibility of the Federal Government of Canada. Immigration levels and the Temporary Foreign Worker Program are controlled by the federal government.

Specific Responsibility

Policy decisions are managed by federal departments and approved by Parliament.

Charter Right Connection

This issue associates to Mobility Rights in the the Canadian Charter of Rights and Freedoms. Mobility rights allow Canadians the freedom to move and work anywhere in Canada. Fair access to job opportunities is necessary to use this right.
