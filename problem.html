Growth of Low-Skill TFWs

The increase in low-skill TFW permits allows companies to hire foreign workers in retail, food service, and entry-level sectors.

When employers rely on lower-cost foreign labor, they may reduce hiring of Canadian youth and delay wage increases.

This can slow wage growth, reduce opportunities for high school students, and increase dependency on low-cost labor instead of innovation.
