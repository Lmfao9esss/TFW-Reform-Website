Introduction

Canada’s Temporary Foreign Worker Program plays an big role in filling labour shortages. However fast growth in low skill permits created an inbalance in the economy affecting high school employment, growth of wages and the housing demand. This website suggests some good changes: reducing overall TFW growth to 2%, limiting low skill worker permits, and prioritizing high skill fields to improve efficiency and long term economic stability.
